Revolutionizing Agricultural Traceability

Ensuring traceability in agricultural produce is a complex challenge for every stakeholder in the supply chain—from farmers in remote locations to procurement hubs, storage facilities, and retail outlets. AgTrace provides a robust solution that streamlines supply chain tracking and ensures full transparency from farm to fork.

End-to-End Supply Chain Visibility

Track every movement of your agricultural products with our comprehensive traceability system that connects all stakeholders in real-time.

Global visibility of operations and supply chain
Near real-time business intelligence
Reduced operational costs

Source Traceability

Verify the origin of every product with our blockchain-backed record-keeping that provides immutable proof of provenance and handling.

Greater transparency for consumers
